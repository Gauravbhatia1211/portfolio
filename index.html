06. Latest Writeups

Medium Jul 15, 2022
Bug Bounty

Ability to Login as Google Staff in Google Cloud Community

Discovered a critical vulnerability that allowed unauthorized access to Google staff privileges in Google Cloud Community platform. The bug bypassed email verification and granted internal staff permissions.

Summary:

While using Google Cloud Community, I discovered that there was a feature of creating an account which we usually don't see in any other Google domains. After creating a post, I noticed there was no email verification process.

Steps to Reproduce:

  1. Go to Google Cloud Community registration page
  2. Create an account with mail id: test@google.com
  3. Account successfully created without email verification
  4. Got privilege of replying to any member as internal Google staff

Impact:

An attacker can login as internal Google staff and spread malicious URLs, files, etc. Also can spread rumors among communities being an internal Google staff which creates negative impression.

Timeline:

  • 2022-02-24: Initial Report to Google VRP
  • 2022-02-24: Issue Triaged
  • 2022-03-09: Internal bug report filed
  • 2022-03-25: VRP issued reward ($100)
